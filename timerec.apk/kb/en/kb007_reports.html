Time Recording

Pull reports
• Go to "Menu / Reports". Alternatively do a long click on the Day, Week or Month button
• Tap one of the reports:
. click the report name (marked red) to send out the report - also see next section
. click the "Preview" icon (marked blue) to preview a report directly on yout phone without sending it out
. each report has detailed settings (tap the icon marked green)


Report output and delivery
• Reports are either delivered as email attachment or uploaded to Google Drive / Dropbox.
• Report output is either "CSV" or "HTML".
. Use "CSV" if you want to import the timesheet data into MS Excel or Open Office
. "HTML" is most convenient for just printing out


CSV trouble shooting
• If CSV import does not work (i.e. all data imported as one cell) try changing the CSV control characters.
• If you're losing special characters (e.g. Umlauts) try changing the "file encoding"
