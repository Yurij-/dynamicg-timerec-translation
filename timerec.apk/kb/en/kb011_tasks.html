Time Recording - Tasks

(A) How to edit tasks

• Click "Switch tasks", then select "Edit". Note the menu on the second screenshot is either opened through the "Menu" button (if your device has one), or by tapping the context icon top right


=> Alternatively, just do a "long click" on the task field on the home screen - this will open the detail edit screen for the given task.
