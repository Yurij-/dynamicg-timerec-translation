Adding entries at midnight or past midnight

Use <<Validity date>> to add entries at or after midnight. Time Recording will keep those work units with the current work day, this allows keeping your work units independent of the calendar day switch.


"Working Late" Preferences
See Preferences on page 4 at the bottom:

• Enable "Show validity date" (red box in picture 1 below) to always have the <<Validity date>> panel displayed on the time picker as shown in picture 2 below.

• Alternatively, the app allows to "roll over" when working cross midnight (i.e. moving to the next calendar day) by enabling the "Roll over" option (picture 1 green box).
When working over midnight this will then:
- add a "Check-Out" at midnight (end of day) on the old day, i.e. closing that day.
- move to next calendar day.
- add a "Check-In" at midnight (start of new day), optionally using the same task.
This way you'll have a seamless work unit transition.
